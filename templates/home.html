CS50 Switch: Wake Up Smarter

Welcome to CS50 Switch — a smart, automatic light switch system designed to help you wake up naturally and comfortably using timed lighting. Rather than waking up to the harsh jolt of a loud alarm, CS50 Switch uses a physical servo motor to turn your room light on at a scheduled time, giving your body and mind time to adjust to the light gradually before you're even out of bed.

The Purpose Behind CS50 Switch

Getting out of bed in the morning can be tough. Especially in dark rooms or colder months, the sudden switch from deep sleep to total wakefulness is not ideal. That’s why CS50 Switch was developed — to provide a seamless transition from sleep to wakefulness by automatically activating your lights 10 minutes before your alarm. This gentle light exposure prepares your brain to wake up more naturally, reducing grogginess and improving mood and mental performance for the rest of the day.

Built for Simplicity, Designed for Purpose

CS50 Switch is a fully self-contained system that connects to your Wi-Fi and operates through a minimal, secure web interface. You can set alarms, toggle the light manually, and check your setup status from any device on your home network. Whether you're a student working on your final project or a tech enthusiast experimenting with home automation, CS50 Switch offers a solid foundation for smart living.

Why You'll Love It

  • Wake Easier: Natural light activation before your alarm.
  • Stress-Free Mornings: No more sudden wake-ups or scrambling to turn on lights.
  • Automated Convenience: Schedule it once and forget it.
  • Built on Real Hardware: Designed for use with ESP32 and servo motors.
  • Built as a CS50 Project: Practical, functional, and fully coded.
